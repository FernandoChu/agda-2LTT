Idea

The {{#concept "dependent universal property of contractible types" Agda=dependent-universal-property-contr}} states that, given a point a : A, the evaluating map

  ev-point a P : ((x : A) ‚Üí P x) ‚Üí P a

is an equivalence for every type family P : A ‚Üí ùí∞.

The condition that ev-point has a section

  P a ‚Üí ((x : A) ‚Üí P x)

is another way of phrasing that the type satisfies singleton induction. Furthermore, the condition that ev-point has a retraction asserts that all dependent functions (x : A) ‚Üí P x are fully determined by their value at a, thus, in particular, that the section of ev-point is unique.
