The map htpy-map-Œ£ preserves homotopies

Given a homotopy H : f ~ f' and a family of dependent homotopies K a : g a ~ g' a over H, expressed as commuting triangles

        g a
   C a -----> D (f a)
      \      /
  g' a \    / tr D (H a)
        ‚à®  ‚à®
      D (f' a)         ,

we get a homotopy htpy-map-Œ£ H K : map-Œ£ f g ~ map-Œ£ f' g'.

This assignment itself preserves homotopies: given H and K as above, H' : f ~ f' with K' a : g a ~ g' a over H', we would like to express coherences between the pairs H, H' and K, K' which would ensure htpy-map-Œ£ H K ~ htpy-map-Œ£ H' K'.   tr D (H a) (g a c) --------------------------------- tr D (H' a) (g a c)
                     \                               /
                        \                         /
                           \                   /
                      K a c   \             /   K' a c
                                 \       /
                                    \ /
                                  g' a c        .
