## Idea

Given a span of maps

```text
      X
     / \
  f /   \ g
   ∨     ∨
  A       B,
```

we define a morphism between the maps in the coslice category of types to be a
map `h : A → B` together with a coherence triangle `(h ∘ f) ~ g`:

```text
      X
     / \
  f /   \ g
   ∨     ∨
  A ----> B.
      h
```
