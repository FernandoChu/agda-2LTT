## Idea

Given a family of pullback squares

```text
    Cᵢ -----> Bᵢ
    | ⌟       |
    |         | gᵢ
    ∨         ∨
    Aᵢ -----> Xᵢ
         fᵢ
```

indexed by `i : I`, their dependent product

```text
  Πᵢ Cᵢ -----> Πᵢ Bᵢ
    | ⌟          |
    |            | Πᵢ gᵢ
    ∨            ∨
  Πᵢ Aᵢ -----> Πᵢ Xᵢ
         Πᵢ fᵢ
```

is again a pullback square.
