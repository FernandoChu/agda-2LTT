Idea

A triangle of morphisms

        top
     x ----> y
      \     /
  left \   / right
        ∨ ∨
         z

in a precategory C is said to commute if there is an identification between:

  left ＝ right ∘ top.

Such a identification is called the {{#concept "coherence" Disambiguation="commuting triangle of morphisms in precategories" Agda=coherence-triangle-hom-Precategory}} of the commuting triangle.
