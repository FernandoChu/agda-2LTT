Idea

A triangle of morphisms

        top
     x ----> y
      \     /
  left \   / right
        ∨ ∨
         z

in a set-magmoid C is said to commute if there is an identification between:

  left ＝ right ∘ top.

Such a identification is called the {{#concept "coherence" Disambiguation="commuting triangle of morphisms in set-magmaoids" Agda=coherence-triangle-hom-Set-Magmoid}} of the commuting triangle.

Definitions

coherence-triangle-hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ :

  {xᵉ yᵉ zᵉ : obj-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ}
  (topᵉ : hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ xᵉ yᵉ)
  (leftᵉ : hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ xᵉ zᵉ)
  (rightᵉ : hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ yᵉ zᵉ) →
  UUᵉ l2ᵉ
coherence-triangle-hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ topᵉ leftᵉ rightᵉ =
  leftᵉ ＝ᵉ comp-hom-Set-Magmoidᵉ Cᵉ rightᵉ topᵉ
